Furnishing in Jaipur

Your local home furnishing specialists

House of Decor is a premium home furnishing showroom in Jagatpura, Jaipur, offering made-to-measure curtains, zebra blinds, roman blinds and fully customised window blinds. We also craft custom wallpaper, sofa upholstery and reupholstery, and supply carpets, carpet tiles, rugs, mosaic glass tiles, ceramic and marble artifacts, rotomold planters and artificial plants — everything that finishes a room.

Every curtain and blind is measured and tailored to your windows. Our team offers a free home measurement and expert installation across Jaipur, including Jagatpura, Pratap Nagar, Malviya Nagar, Mansarovar, Raja Park, Jawahar Nagar, Vaishali Nagar, C-Scheme, Tonk Road and surrounding areas. Visit our Central Spine showroom to see fabrics and finishes in person, or book a consultation and we'll bring the swatches to you.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Do you offer free measurement at home?

Yes. We visit your home anywhere in Jaipur with swatches, measure your windows exactly, and there's no charge for the measurement visit.

What types of blinds do you make?

Zebra blinds, roman blinds, roller blinds and fully customised blinds, all made to your window size and fitted by our team.

Where is your showroom located?

We're at Central Spine, Jagatpura, Jaipur. Open Monday to Saturday, 11 AM to 8 PM, and Sundays by appointment.

Do you deliver across Jaipur?

Yes, we deliver and install across Jaipur and nearby areas. Smaller decor pieces like artifacts and planters can be ordered on WhatsApp.
